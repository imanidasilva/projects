About Training

1 on 1 training and group training are located at Inspire Fitness. This is a 24/7 hour gym so you will have access to the gym outside of my classes. You will have to get a gym membership which is $20. No contract, just month-to-month payments. Before joining let gym staff know you are training with me and they will waive the enrollment fee. The gym membership cost is not included in the group or 1 on 1 training.

Disclaimer

For beginners who have never worked out or haven't worked out in a while, it is recommended to sign up for 1 on 1 training. This is where we can perfect your form and prepare you for the fast pace group training. I offer 30 minutes, 45 minutes, and 60 minutes sessions.
